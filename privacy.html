Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

SnapReceipt is a UK-based service. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about how your data is handled.

What we collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide the service:

  • Your email address (to create and manage your account)
  • Receipt data you choose to scan and save — merchant names, amounts, dates, categories and any notes you add
  • Receipt images, stored to display thumbnails in your account

How we use it

Your data is used solely to provide the SnapReceipt service to you. We never sell your data, share it with third parties, or use it for advertising.

Where it's stored

Your account data and receipts are stored on Supabase, which uses bank-level encryption and is hosted in the EU.

Receipt images are processed by Anthropic's Claude AI to extract text (merchant, amount, date). Images are sent securely to Anthropic's API for this purpose and are not permanently stored by Anthropic.

Email forwarding

If you use the email forwarding feature (forwarding receipts to receipts@snapreceipt.co.uk), the content of those emails is processed to extract receipt data and stored in your account. We do not read, share or retain email content beyond what is needed to log the receipt.

How long we keep it

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. HMRC requires you to keep business records for at least 6 years — SnapReceipt keeps them safe for you.

You can delete individual receipts at any time. To delete your account and all associated data, email hello@snapreceipt.co.uk and we'll action it within 30 days.

Your rights (GDPR)

As a UK/EU user you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data (use the CSV export in the app)
  • Object to processing

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@snapreceipt.co.uk.

Cookies

SnapReceipt uses only essential session cookies required for authentication. No tracking, analytics or advertising cookies are used.

Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Your account is protected by password authentication. We recommend using a strong, unique password and enabling PIN lock in the app settings.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy we'll update the date above and, where appropriate, notify you by email.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@snapreceipt.co.uk
