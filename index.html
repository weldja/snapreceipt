Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

SnapReceipt is a UK-based service. We take your privacy seriously.

What we collect
Your email address (for your account), and the receipt data you choose to scan and save — merchant names, amounts, dates and any notes you add. Receipt images may be stored to display thumbnails.

How we use it
Solely to provide the SnapReceipt service to you. Your data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

Where it's stored
Your data is stored on Supabase, which uses bank-level encryption and is hosted in the EU. Receipt images are processed by Anthropic's Claude AI to extract text — images are not stored by Anthropic.

How long we keep it
For as long as your account is active. You can delete individual receipts at any time. To delete your account and all data, email [email protected].

Your rights (GDPR)
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact [email protected] to exercise these rights.

Cookies
SnapReceipt uses only essential session cookies required for authentication. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.
