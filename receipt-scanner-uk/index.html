FAQ

Common questions

Is SnapReceipt free?
Yes, completely free during early access. There are no limits on the number of receipts you can scan.
Does it extract VAT from receipts?
Yes. SnapReceipt reads the VAT amount and supplier VAT number automatically from receipts where shown — alongside merchant name, total, and date. No manual entry needed.
Does it work for Making Tax Digital?
Yes. MTD for Income Tax requires sole traders earning over £50,000 to keep digital records from April 2026. SnapReceipt's digital receipt storage satisfies HMRC's MTD record-keeping requirements. Read our MTD guide →
Do I need to download an app?
No. SnapReceipt works in your mobile browser on iPhone and Android — nothing to install. You can add it to your home screen for one-tap access.
How long are receipts stored?
Receipts are stored securely for as long as you need them. HMRC requires sole traders to keep records for at least 5 years after the Self Assessment deadline — SnapReceipt's storage is built around this. See HMRC's record-keeping rules →
Can I export receipts for my accountant?
Yes. Export all receipts as a CSV at any time — categorised and ready to hand straight to your accountant or use for your Self Assessment return.
